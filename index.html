The Real Problem

Fault Detection is Broken

Today's process: Engineers manually review A429 logs, write custom scripts, and hope they catch critical faults. IRS velocity divergence? GPS position blunders? Good luck finding them in time.

The Data Deluge

A single 737 flight generates 100GB+ of avionics data. A429 buses stream at 100KHz. IRS data at 10Hz. GPS at 1Hz. Manual analysis is impossible. Critical faults get missed.

Certification Nightmare

DO-178C requires exhaustive fault analysis. Current tools? Spreadsheets, custom scripts, and hope. Each certification cycle costs millions in manual V&V. Delays cost more.
